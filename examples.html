Sequence Animations

Create staggered animations with sequences.

1
2
3
<div vfx-sequence vfx-sequence-delay="200">
    <div vfx-animate="fade-in">1</div>
    <div vfx-animate="fade-in">2</div>
    <div vfx-animate="fade-in">3</div>
</div>
1
2
3
<div vfx-sequence vfx-sequence-delay="200" vfx-sequence-direction="reverse">
    <div vfx-animate="fade-in">1</div>
    <div vfx-animate="fade-in">2</div>
    <div vfx-animate="fade-in">3</div>
</div>

Parallax Effects

Add depth with parallax scrolling effects.

Slow (0.5x) 
vfx-parallax="0.5"
Fast (1.5x) 
vfx-parallax="1.5"
3D Effect 
vfx-parallax="1" vfx-parallax-perspective="1000"
